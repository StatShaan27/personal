Weekly Class Structure

Classes each week are divided into two focused sessions:
1) Concept Building — understanding fundamentals clearly.
2) Problem Solving — applying concepts to structured exercises.

Topic-wise lecture slides and practice materials will be uploaded regularly. Every two weeks, students receive structured assignments, and every four weeks, a cumulative exam is conducted.
